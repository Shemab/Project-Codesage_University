About Us

Welcome to Code Sage University , situated in Assam, where excellence meets education. As a premier institution offering a diverse range of courses, including technology and management, we take pride in being a beacon of academic brilliance. Boasting a remarkable 100% placement rate, we have forged strong ties with international MNCs, ensuring our graduates step confidently into the professional world. Our distinguished faculty hails from around the globe, enriching learning with their expertise. At CodeSage, we are committed to nurturing future leaders and innovators. Join us to explore boundless opportunities for learning, growth, and success.
