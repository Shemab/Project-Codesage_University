Welcome to CodeSage University Admission 2023

  1. Admission to all Under-Graduate Programs of UoD for the Academic Session 2023-24 will be through CS-SET(UG)-2023, based on the eligibility requirements stated in UG ADM-2023 and other rules as published by the Code Sage University on its website.

  2. Admission to all UG Programs of UoD will be done through this portal on the basis of the University Entrance Test- Under-Graduate 2023 (CS-SET (UG) – 2023).

  3. The Common Seat Allocation System (UG) 2023 includes: APPLYING TO CODE SAGE UNIVERSITY FILLING THE PREFRENCES FOR PROGRAMS AND COLLEGES ALLOCATION-CUM-ADMISSION

  4. CATEGORY CS-SET(UG)-2023 Application Fee
    UR/OBC-NCL/EWS Rs. 250.00 (Rupees Two Hundred Fifty only)
    SC/ST/PwBD/GEN Rs. 100.00 (Rupees Hundred only)

    Applying to ECA and Sports supernumerary quota, there is an additional fee of Rs.100.00 (Rupees Hundred only) for each quota.

  5. Candidates must check their dashboard, email, and admission website ( www.admission.uod.ac.in) for all communications, updates and schedules related to all the Phases of CSAS(UG)-2023.
