Programs & Courses:


Following Undergraduate and Postgraduate Courses offered in Code Sage University

The college is affiliated to Ranchi University, Ranchi. It offers Undergraduate (B.A./B.Com/B.Sc. Honours), Postgraduate (M.A./M.C.A./M.J.M.C) Courses under the faculty of Arts, Commerce & Science. Besides this it also offers Professional, Vocational & Management programmes

Admission deadline for postgraduate courses: September 19th. Inquiries or assistance needed, ask within this period.
